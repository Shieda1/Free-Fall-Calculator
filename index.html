What is the free fall?
Free fall is the exclusive movement of the body under the influence of the earth's gravity.
How to calculate free fall?
To calculate free fall you should use the following formula:
vf = g * t
where:
g = acceleration of gravity = 9.81 m/s^2
t = time
Why use free fall?
The free fall calculator is used in order to measure the movement of the body under the influence of the earth's gravity.
